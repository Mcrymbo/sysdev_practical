Calculation Formula

Tonnage (MT) = (Volume × Density × VCF) ÷ 1000

• Volume: Measured in litres

• Density: Measured in kg/m³ (rounded to nearest 0.5)

• Temperature: Measured in °C (rounded to nearest 0.25)

• VCF: Volume Correction Factor from lookup table

• Result: Tonnage in Metric Tonnes (MT)
